# Eldar

`рЯІЩвАНвЩВпЄП Character`

A person who has been around since anyone would remember. Just a human like any other.

Used to be part of [Cybernetics Inc](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/cybernetics_inc.html>) before deciding to retire from the faction due to the pressure of battles.

Eventually had the idea to become a neutral reckoning force; a sellsword that defends and attacks as they will.

Became part of the [Fighters Guild](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/fighters_guild.html>) as one of its founders; a place to bond with sellswords and make a profit of the heated situations that the factions have inevitably found themselves in.

Was part of the rebel team that invaded [MindTech Institute](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/mindtech_institute.html>) [Timeless Desert](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/timeless_desert.html>) as a first example of a rebellion brewing in a faction.

Became a one-man mighty force that ruined [MindTech Institute](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/mindtech_institute.html>)'s plans, rivaled [Delta Collective](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/delta_collective.html>)'s performance and feared by many personnel.

Was one of the founders of [Cloudy Operatives](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/cloudy_operatives.html>).

Set to retire after achieving #1 in the Hall of Fame being a veteran of 99 battles.
