# King's Challenges

`📑 Term`

An ancient tradition of selecting a new [King of Zeithalt](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/king_of_zeithalt.html>).

The Challenge is a series of tests and trials that the candidates must pass to prove their worthiness to rule.

However, some speculate that in the end, the winner is chosen randomly.

Since the creation of the [Royal Guard](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/royal_guard.html>), this faction has overseen the process of selecting and crowning the next [King](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/king_of_zeithalt.html>).
