## Mysterious Mazes

`🪧 Place`

A network of underground tunnels of mysterious origin.

Entrances to them are occasionally found across the Island. While they appear welcoming at first, once you step inside, the entrance seals shut, leaving the traveller in complete darkness.

These mazes often contain [great rewards](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/amazeing_mindblade.html>) and are home to previously unseen creatures.

Eventually, the [Cartographer's Guild](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/cartographers_guild_hq.html>) was established to investigate, map, and uncover the secrets of these mazes.
