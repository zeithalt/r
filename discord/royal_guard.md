## Royal Guard

`🪪 Faction`

Royal Guard is a faction dedicated to the purpose of serving the current [King of Zeithalt](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/king_of_zeithalt.html>).

A new [King](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/king_of_zeithalt.html>) is chosen every once in a while via the [King’s Challenge](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/kings_challenges.html>).

The faction fighting on the [King's](<https://zeithalt.github.io/r/king_of_zeithalt.html>), orders in battles, however, tries to keep a neutral stance towards the other factions.
